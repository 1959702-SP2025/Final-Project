About Sprout & Grow

Located in Raleigh, North Carolina, Sprout & Grow is a locally owned greenhouse nursery dedicated to providing high-quality organic plants and empowering communities through sustainable gardening.

We believe in transparency, ethical growing practices, and ecological responsibility. Our plants are nurtured without harmful chemicals and grown with the environment in mind.

Our Values

  • Environmental stewardship
  • Education and community involvement
  • Healthy, organic plant cultivation
  • Accessibility for beginner and experienced gardeners alike
